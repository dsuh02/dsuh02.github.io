    CovidHacks

    Covid-19 is a calamity that is affecting everyone, indiscriminate to age, nationality, or anything that divides us -- that's why CovidHacks was created. CovidHacks is a hackathon that aims to draw youth programming enthusiasts from all around the globe together to develop innovative solutions to the challenges caused by the COVID-19 experience. This event took place for 24 hours, and it was open to students of all skill levels.

    Over these 24 hours, 3 different workshops were hosted for any participants who wished to attend:
    “Build a Cloud-Connected AR/VR App in 15 Minutes or Less” by EchoAR
    "Intro to Firebase Client” by STAC Programming
    “How to be Financially Literate” by FLEO

    Images above: Opening Ceremony, Sponsors List, Prizes List, Closing Ceremony, DEVPOST Page.

    Over the 24 hours, 315 participants internationally submitted 54 projects. Of these participants, the 3 top teams as well as 2 other teams winning the EchoAR challenge and the Sashido Machine Learning challenge were showcased and awarded. Finally, we held a raffle with 7 prizes open to all participants. I was in charge of implenting a visual raffle system with front-end programming.

    Agend-Aid

    The Problem:
    Many instructors have a problem with keeping all of their work-related applications in one place, often leading to lower efficiency and issues performing daily tasks like sending out students to the bathroom or the office.

    The App:
    This application has all the necessary features that could accompany the instructor besides existing registrar applications like Powerschool. This would allow less actions to be performed in order to organize the day for the many classes an instructor has.

    Agend-Aid Features:

    -Daily agenda plan for each class.
    -Class attendance by period.
    -The ability to assign and receive hall passes which cuts out wasted time for office TA.
    -VITAL: allows swift and silent communication between campus security and staff while not alarming possible perpetrators.
    -Confidential login for instructors, staff, and campus security.
    -Helpful information for staff and students (Emergency info, School Map, Phone #’s, etc.)
